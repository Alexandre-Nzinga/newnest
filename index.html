The Kuwait of Africa

Angola is a country in Southern Africa known for its rich cultural heritage and diverse natural landscapes. It is a country of contrasts, blending traditional customs with modern influences. It's economy is driven by its vast natural resources, including oil, diamonds, and minerals. Angola's business environment is characterized by its formal hierarchical structure and relationship-based culture.



Understanding proper business etiquette is crucial for success. Angola shows high power distance, strong collectivism, and moderate uncertainty avoidance, reflecting its traditional social structures and community-oriented values. Navigating daily life in Angola requires an understanding of local customs and practical considerations, such as transportation, accommodation, healthcare, and more.
